About Me

Hi! My name is Dalton Craven. I'm an 11th grader at Mason High School. I've been interested in computers my whole life, and taking programming my freshman year helped me decide that was what I wanted to do. I'm currently working with the Mason ACM as their official webmaster. I'm also in Mason Hack Club as a web designer and game programmer. I'm also learning basic server management & web design through the Integrated Media Internship program, and I've interned with the City of Mason's IT team to get some hands-on experience in a real-life workplace. Outside of school, I like working on my 1963 Mercury Comet, reading, and working at Kings Island.
