About Me

Hi! My name is Dalton Craven. I'm a 10th grader at Mason High School. I've been interested in computers my whole life, and taking programming my freshman year helped me decide that was what I wanted to do. I'm currently working with the Mason ACM as their official webmaster. I'm also in Mason Hack Club as a web designer and game programmer. I'm also learning basic server management & web design through the Integrated Media Internship program, and I've interned with the City of Mason's IT team to get some hands-on experience in a real-life workplace. Outside of school, I'm a staff member on a Minecraft server called SuperFunTime. I also own a 1963 Mercury Comet Custom that I enjoy working on with my free time.
