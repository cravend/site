## About

I believe that coding is about creating meaningful experiences that empower people — regardless of how they communicate or where they come from. I've developed with a wide-ranging toolkit (TypeScript, React, Python, and more) but my core focus remains the same: building products that don't exclude users due to language or ability.

That's why I'm drawn to localization and accessibility — two areas where small changes can have an enormous impact. Influenced by my background in English, French, and American Sign Language, I'm convinced that a truly equitable internet must speak the user's language in every sense. By pushing for accessible standards and “de-Englishing” the web, I hope to help shape a future where technology actually reflects the diversity of its users.
